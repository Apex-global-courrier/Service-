About Apex Global Courrier Service

We are committed to delivering exceptional shipping services that are reliable, safe, and cost-effective. With over 29 years in the logistics industry, we have become a trusted name across five continents.

Founded in 1995 in South Australia, we have since expanded to 47 head offices worldwide — South America, Europe, Asia, and Africa — providing end-to-end logistics solutions backed by a global network of partners.

Frequently Asked Questions

What type of cargo can Apex Global handle?
We handle a wide range of cargo including documents, parcels, and pallets. We also specialise in the transportation of dangerous or hazardous goods with full regulatory compliance.
Does Apex Global provide insurance for my shipment?
Yes, we offer comprehensive cargo insurance. Coverage terms apply — please consult our customer service team for a tailored policy to suit your shipment.
What is the cost of shipping with Apex Global?
Cost depends on weight, destination, mode of transportation, and shipment type. We offer competitive rates and provide customised quotes — contact us for an assessment.
Does Apex Global provide real-time tracking?
Yes — all shipments include real-time tracking. You can track via our website or by contacting our customer service team at any time.
Can I change or cancel my shipment?
Yes, changes or cancellations may be made. Applicable charges depend on the stage of shipment and the service selected. Contact us as early as possible.
What if my shipment is lost or damaged?
We take every precaution to ensure safe delivery. In the unlikely event of loss or damage, our comprehensive claims process ensures fair and timely compensation.
Does Apex Global handle customs clearance?
Yes — our expert team ensures full customs compliance for every international shipment, making the clearance process smooth and hassle-free.

1Service Scope

Apex Global Courrier Service provides domestic and international courier, logistics, and freight services. By booking with us, customers agree to these terms.

2Booking and Payment

  • Orders must be placed through our official website or authorized agents.
  • Payment is required before dispatch unless otherwise agreed in writing.
  • International shipments may incur customs duties, taxes, or fees payable by the recipient.

3Delivery and Tracking

  • Tracking numbers are provided for all shipments.
  • Delivery timelines are estimates and may vary due to customs clearance, international regulations, or unforeseen delays.
  • We are not liable for delays caused by customs authorities or external factors beyond our control.

4Liabilities

  • We are responsible for parcels only while in our custody.
  • Claims for loss or damage must be filed within 7 days of delivery.

5Prohibited Items

  • We do not ship hazardous materials, illegal goods, counterfeit products, or perishable items.
  • Shipments violating international trade laws will be confiscated and reported to authorities.

6Cancellation and Refunds

  • Orders canceled before dispatch may qualify for partial refunds.
  • No refunds are available once a parcel is in transit.
  • Customs-related delays or fees are non-refundable.

7Data Protection

  • Customer data is used solely for service fulfillment.
  • We comply with GDPR and other international privacy regulations.
  • Data is never shared with third parties without consent.

8Governing Law

These terms are governed by the UN, EU and applicable international trade regulations.

Privacy Policy

How Apex Global Courrier Service collects, uses, and protects your data.

🔒 Effective for all customers using our website, agents, and services

1Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to process bookings and deliver our services, including:

  • Contact details such as name, email address, phone number, and shipping/billing address.
  • Shipment details including parcel contents, weight, destination, and customs documentation.
  • Payment information processed securely through our payment partners.
  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, and cookies collected when you use our website or tracking tools.

2How We Use Your Information

Your data is used strictly to fulfill and support our services, including:

  • Processing bookings, generating tracking numbers, and coordinating delivery.
  • Completing customs clearance and regulatory documentation for international shipments.
  • Responding to customer service inquiries and resolving complaints.
  • Sending shipment updates, service notices, and — where consented — promotional communications.

3Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell customer data. Information is only shared where necessary to deliver our services:

  • With carrier, customs, and logistics partners strictly to complete delivery and clearance.
  • With payment processors to complete transactions securely.
  • With regulatory or government authorities where required by law, such as customs declarations.
  • Never with third parties for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

4Data Security and Retention

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted transmission of payment and personal data.
  • Restricted internal access to customer information on a need-to-know basis.
  • Retention of shipment and account data only as long as necessary for service, legal, or tax purposes, after which it is securely deleted.

5Your Rights and Choices

In line with GDPR and other applicable international privacy regulations, you have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.
  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe your data has been mishandled.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info.agcs@gmail.com.
